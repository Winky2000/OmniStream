Templates

Templates are stored in config.json under newsletterTemplates and are used by the Subscribers page when sending newsletters.

Editor & preview

Newsletter logo

Use {{NEWSLETTER_LOGO_BLOCK}} in HTML templates to insert the logo only when configured. Or use {{NEWSLETTER_LOGO_URL}} inside your own <img src>.
Changes are not saved until you click "Save template". Placeholders supported: {{START_DATE}}, {{END_DATE}}, {{RECENTLY_ADDED}}, {{CUSTOM_SECTIONS}}, {{NEWSLETTER_LOGO_URL}}, {{NEWSLETTER_LOGO_BLOCK}}. Custom header blocks are managed on the Custom header page. If you omit {{CUSTOM_SECTIONS}}, OmniStream will auto-insert your custom blocks near the top (under the header/logo) for HTML templates. If the body contains <html>/<!DOCTYPE html>, OmniStream sends it as HTML email.
